Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2025

Welcome to EventHive! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and account details.
  • Event Information: Details of events you create or register for.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • To manage your account and provide personalized event recommendations.
  • To improve our platform and user experience.
  • To communicate with you regarding events, updates, and promotions.
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activities.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or trade your personal information. However, we may share your data with:

  • Service providers assisting with payment processing and analytics.
  • Legal authorities if required by law.

4. Data Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no online service is completely secure.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal data. To request changes, contact us at support@eventhive.com.

7. Changes to This Policy
